# Privacy

Is Your Data Safe? is a static directory. It practices what it preaches:

- **No accounts.** There is nothing to sign up for.
- **No analytics or tracking scripts.** We don't measure you.
- **No cookies.** The site sets none.
- **No data collection.** Searching and filtering happen entirely in your
  browser; no queries are sent to a server.

The only data the site handles is the public review data in the
[directory spreadsheet](README.md#link-your-spreadsheet), rendered at build
time. Submissions happen through GitHub issues (governed by
[GitHub's privacy policy](https://docs.github.com/en/site-policy/privacy-policies))
or by email, at your choice.
